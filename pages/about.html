About

Helping students understand their grades clearly

Degree Grade Tracker is a free student tool that helps you calculate your module averages, year averages, final degree grade, and target grades in a simple way.

Why this website exists

University grade calculations can be confusing, especially when different modules, assessments, and academic years all have different weightings.

This website was built to make that process easier. Instead of manually calculating every module and year percentage, students can enter their grades and instantly see an estimated final result.

What you can do with it

Calculate module grades

Add assessments such as exams, coursework, presentations, or projects with their own weights.

Track year averages

See your Year 2 and Year 3 averages based on the modules you add.

Change year weighting

Adjust the Year 2 and Year 3 percentages so the calculator works with different universities.

Check target grades

See what average you may need to achieve your target degree classification.

Who it is for

This tool is mainly designed for UK university students who want a quick and simple way to estimate their final degree classification.

It can be useful for students aiming for a First, 2:1, 2:2, or simply trying to understand how much each year and assessment contributes to their final result.

Important note

Degree Grade Tracker gives an estimate only. Universities may use different grading rules, rounding methods, compensation rules, credits, or classification policies.

Always check your official university regulations or speak to your course team if you need an exact final grade calculation.

Privacy first

The grades and module details you enter are saved locally in your browser. They are not sent to a server, and the website does not require an account.

You can clear your saved data at any time using the clear button in the calculator.
