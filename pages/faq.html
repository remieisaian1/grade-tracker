Help & Contact

Frequently asked questions

Find answers about how the calculator works, how your data is stored, and how to contact us.

Does this calculator work for every university?

It can work for many universities because you can manually change the Year 2 and Year 3 weighting. However, every university may have different rules, so this should be used as an estimate only.

What does year weighting mean?

Year weighting is how much each academic year contributes to your final degree grade. For example, some universities use 25% for Year 2 and 75% for Year 3.

Are my grades saved online?

No. Your grades are saved only in your browser using local storage. They are not sent to a server and no account is needed.

Can I clear my saved grades?

Yes. Use the “clear all” button on the calculator page. You can also clear your browser data.

Why is my final grade only an estimate?

Some universities use extra rules such as credit weighting, rounding, compensation, borderline rules, or special classification methods. Always check your official university regulations.

Can I use this for Year 1?

The current version focuses on Year 2 and Year 3 because these are commonly used for final degree classification. If your university includes Year 1, you can adjust the percentages or wait for a future version.
