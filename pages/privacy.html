Privacy Policy for Degree Grade Tracker

Last updated: June 2026

Degree Grade Tracker is designed to help students calculate and estimate their university grades. Your privacy is important, and this page explains what information is stored and how it is used.

1. Information we collect

This website does not ask you to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, student ID, or university login details.

The grades, module names, assessment names, assessment weights, and year weightings you enter are saved only in your own browser using local storage. This allows the calculator to remember your data when you come back to the website on the same device and browser.

2. Local storage

Degree Grade Tracker uses your browser’s local storage to save your calculator data. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our server.

You can remove this data at any time by clicking the “clear all” button inside the calculator, or by clearing your browser data.

3. Google AdSense and advertising

In the future, this website may use Google AdSense or other advertising services to display ads. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to show relevant adverts and measure ad performance.

Third-party advertising providers may collect information such as your browser type, device information, pages visited, and general usage data. This does not include the grades or module information you enter into the calculator.

4. Cookies

This website itself does not currently use cookies for user accounts or login systems. However, third-party services such as advertising or analytics providers may use cookies if they are added to the website.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Analytics

This website may use analytics tools in the future to understand how visitors use the site and improve the user experience. Analytics data is usually collected in an aggregated way and does not personally identify you.

6. Your data

Because your grade data is stored locally in your own browser, we cannot view, edit, recover, or delete it for you. You are in control of the data stored on your device.

7. External links

This website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of other websites.

8. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be shown on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

Degree Grade Tracker is an independent student tool. Always check your official university regulations.
