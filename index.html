How to use the calculator

  1. Add your modules for each year.
  2. Enter the credits for each module.
  3. Add each assessment with its weight and grade.
  4. Adjust the Year 2 and Year 3 weighting if needed.
  5. Check your estimated final grade and targets.

If you do not know your credits, keep the same credit value for each module.
